## Post 2 - June 9, 2026
**Topic:** If You Are at Scale, You Now Have 2 Full-Time Jobs

Every B2B CEO at $50M+ ARR now has two full-time jobs:

Job 1: Keep your installed base happy. 
Job 2: Win the AI agent war in your space.

Both are full-time. Neither is optional. And they pull in opposite directions.

🤲 Job 1 means protecting NRR, which means your existing customers are getting value, renewals are holding, and expansion is happening. This requires deep attention to the current product, current workflows, current customer needs. 

Median NRR across B2B software right now is 108-110%. Top quartile is 123%+. The gap between those two numbers is the difference between compounding growth and slow decline.

🤖 Job 2 means building the AI agent that wins your category before someone else does. Not an AI feature. Not a chatbot. The actual agent that does the job your software currently helps humans do. If you sell CRM, it is the agent that runs the entire pipeline. If you sell support software, it is the agent that resolves 80% of tickets autonomously.

The problem: Job 2 requires cannibalizing Job 1. Your VP of Product will resist it because it threatens the core product roadmap. Your engineering team will say they do not have capacity. Your sales team will be confused about what to sell. Your CS team will worry about workflow disruption.

These are rational concerns. They are also the antibodies that will kill you.
At SaaStr, we run 20+ AI agents. Exactly one comes from a legacy vendor (Salesforce's Agentforce). The other 19+ are from AI-native companies. Legacy vendors had every advantage. Distribution, data, customer trust. Most squandered it.

The CEO job has not been this hard in 15+ years. CEO resignations are meaningfully up. The only answer for those who stay: do both jobs fully.

Grow or die. That is the 2026 version.
